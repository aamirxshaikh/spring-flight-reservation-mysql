Pack correctly for safety

That is why it is important that before your departure you inform yourself thoroughly and pack correctly. Have a first aid kit with the basics, especially pain medication


Sometimes flights can cause headaches, so have a pack ready just in case this happens to be that one. A few things you may want to pack: Painkillers, Bandages. A sedative (if you are a nervous traveler) Anti-nausea medication, Chewing gum (for air pressure changes), Tissues Earplugs (good for travel in general), Medication for anything you are prone to, such as allergies.
